Land Management

Staff Accountant – Land Management

Cumming, Iowa  ·  Full-Time

Overview

This role manages accounting operations for Peoples Company's Land and Asset Management divisions, reporting to the Controller. The position involves processing financial transactions, maintaining asset records, and preparing financial statements while communicating with clients and leadership.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage accounts payable, accounts receivable, cash receipts, journal entries, account reconciliations, and depreciation schedules
  • Track assets across multiple entities and maintain accurate records
  • Prepare general ledger reconciliations and financial statements at various intervals
  • Analyze data to identify and implement process improvements

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in accounting, or an associate's degree plus five years of relevant experience
  • 2–3 years of accounting experience, preferably covering accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger reconciliations
  • Proficiency in accounting software (Premier, QuickBooks); Yardi experience preferred
  • Advanced Excel skills
  • Strong attention to detail with excellent verbal and written communication
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively, and to meet tight deadlines

Work Expectations

Standard office hours are 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday. Team members are expected to attend at least 90% of company-sponsored meetings and events.
