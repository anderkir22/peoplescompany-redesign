Our Story

From a Single Iowa Bank to the Nation's Largest Farmland Firm

Every acre we manage, every transaction we close, is part of a journey that began in the American heartland and grew into a national institution.

1970s
The Beginning

Rooted in an Iowa Farm Bank

Peoples Company traces its origins to the agricultural banking and land advisory traditions of rural Iowa — a region where families understood that land wasn't simply an asset, it was a legacy. Early advisors built relationships one farm, one family at a time.

1990s
Foundation

A Dedicated Farmland Brokerage is Born

Formalizing decades of farm advisory work, Peoples Company emerges as a standalone farmland brokerage — one of the first in the region to combine auction expertise with traditional private-sale brokerage and active land management under one roof.

Early Iowa farmland brokerage
2000s
Milestone · Expansion

Growing Beyond Iowa — Regional Offices Open

Demand from landowners across the Corn Belt drives rapid geographic expansion. New regional offices open across Illinois, Nebraska, Kansas, and Indiana, bringing Peoples Company's proven methodology to farmland owners in new markets.

20+ Regional Offices today, coast to coast
2010s
Diversification

Integrated Land Solutions Take Shape

Recognizing that landowners and investors need more than a transaction, Peoples Company builds out its full suite: Agricultural Appraisal, Land Management, Capital Markets, Energy Management, Crop Insurance, and Corporate Services — seven disciplines united under one roof.

2015
Milestone · Capital

$1 Billion in Farmland Transactions Closed

A major inflection point — crossing the billion-dollar threshold validates both the scale of American farmland as an asset class and the trust landowners and investors place in Peoples Company as their platform of choice.

$6B+ Total farmland transactions to date
2017
Community

The Land Investment EXPO Launches

The firm creates the nation's premier gathering for farmland investors — a forum where institutional capital, family offices, and landowners meet to exchange research, discover opportunities, and shape the conversation around agricultural real estate.

1.6K+ Annual EXPO attendees
2020s
Milestone · People

170+ Agricultural Professionals Nationwide

The team expands to include licensed agents, certified appraisers, farm managers, energy consultants, and capital markets advisors across the United States — the largest dedicated farmland professional workforce of any single firm.

170+ Ag professionals on the Peoples Company platform
2023
Technology

FarmWorth™ — Proprietary Farmland Intelligence

The firm launches FarmWorth, a proprietary data platform providing automated appraisal modeling, soil capability analysis, comparable-sales databases, and data layers — giving every client access to institutional-grade intelligence for their land decisions.

FarmWorth™ platform UI — parcels, data layers, soil analysis
