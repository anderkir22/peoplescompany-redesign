About Peoples Company

Peoples Company is the largest farmland brokerage and management firm in the United States — a national platform built on trust, data, and generations of agricultural knowledge. From a single Iowa office to more than twenty regional locations, the firm has grown alongside the American land market, never losing its roots.

Our advisors work with landowners who have held their ground for generations, with farm operators seeking productive long-term leases, and with sophisticated investors who understand that productive farmland offers a rare combination of income, appreciation, and resilience.

Every service we offer — from brokerage and auctions to capital markets and energy management — is designed to deliver value at every stage of land ownership.

What sets Peoples Company apart is integration. A landowner can sell, manage, insure, and appraise their property through one relationship, backed by FarmWorth™, the firm's proprietary data platform providing institutional-grade analysis for every acre.

Markets

Farmland Values Remain Resilient Through 2026

Midwest farmland markets have held firm against macroeconomic headwinds, with strong demand from both institutional and individual buyers. Low supply of quality row-crop ground and sustained operator income underpin values across the Corn Belt.

Peoples Company's FarmWorth™ platform tracks comparable-sale data across 2,000+ counties, giving clients an edge in competitive markets and in private negotiations.

"Quality farmland is one of the most durable stores of value in the American economy."
— 2025 National Land Values Report, Peoples Company

Renewable energy leases — solar and wind — are adding a new income layer to agricultural land, increasingly affecting per-acre value calculations in transitional markets.

Research

Why Family Offices Are Turning to Farmland

The alignment between farmland's characteristics — low correlation to equities, income-producing, inflation-hedged — and the long-duration mandates of family offices and endowments has driven sustained institutional interest.

Peoples Company's Capital Markets practice connects family offices, institutional funds, private equity, and Ag REITs with high-quality farmland assets, leveraging the firm's national reach and proprietary comparable-sales data.

"Farmland offers what most alternative assets cannot: genuine scarcity, embedded income, and generational staying power."
— Peoples Company Capital Markets

The Land Investment EXPO, held annually, brings together over 1,600 institutional and individual attendees for research presentations, networking, and deal flow — a unique forum for the farmland investment community.
